About

Welcome to radio station More Oldies where we began broadcasting on Oct. 1, 2022 and operates 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.
We play the very best pop, rock, dance and soul tunes ranging from the 1950's to the 1990's.

Be sure to check out 'The Sunday Night Hall of Fame'with DJ Stone every Sunday night from 10pm - 1am EDT (US Time)
when he plays your favorite hits from the 50's, 60's and 70's. To watch the live video feed just select 'DJ Stone Live' from the site menu.
